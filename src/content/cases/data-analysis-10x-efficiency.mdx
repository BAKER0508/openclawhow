## The Pain Point

Jenny is a data analyst at a mid-sized marketing agency. Every Monday, she had to manually pull data from 6 different platforms (Google Analytics, Meta Ads, Shopify, Mailchimp, HubSpot, and Google Sheets), compile it into a report, and present it to 8 clients. This took **15-20 hours per week** — nearly half her working time.

## The Automation

Using OpenClaw's **Browser Skill** and **Data Skill**, Jenny built an automated reporting pipeline:

- **Data Collection**: The Browser Skill logs into each platform and extracts key metrics every Monday at 6 AM
- **Data Processing**: The Data Skill normalizes everything into a unified format, calculates week-over-week changes, and identifies anomalies
- **Report Generation**: A templated report is generated for each client with charts, insights, and recommended actions
- **Delivery**: Reports are emailed to clients automatically by 9 AM

## Impact

- Report generation time: **20 hours/week down to 2 hours/week** (just review and minor edits)
- **10x efficiency gain** overall
- Reports are now **more consistent** — no more copy-paste errors
- Clients receive reports **3 hours earlier** than before
- Jenny now has time for **deep analysis work** that actually adds strategic value

## Technical Notes

- The agent runs on a scheduled cron job every Monday
- Each platform integration took about 30 minutes to set up
- The hardest part was getting the chart formatting right — took an extra day of tweaking
- Total setup time: about 12 hours spread over a week

> "I went from dreading Mondays to actually looking forward to them. The reports basically write themselves now, and I can focus on the interesting analytical work."

## 痛点

## 自动化方案

Jenny使用OpenClaw的**浏览器技能**和**数据技能**搭建了一套自动化报告流水线：

- **数据采集**：浏览器技能每周一早上6点自动登录各平台并提取关键指标
- **数据处理**：数据技能将所有数据统一格式化，计算周环比变化，识别异常
- **报告生成**：为每个客户生成模板化报告，包含图表、洞察和行动建议
- **报告分发**：报告在早上9点前自动发送到客户邮箱

## 成效

- 报告生成时间：**从每周20小时降至每周2小时**（只需审阅和小幅修改）

- 报告**更加一致**——不再有复制粘贴错误
- 客户**提前3小时**收到报告
- Jenny现在有时间做**深度分析**，真正创造战略价值

- Agent通过定时任务在每周一自动运行
