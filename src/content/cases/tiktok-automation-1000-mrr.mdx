## The Story

Oliver Henry is a solo creator who wanted to scale his TikTok presence without spending 8 hours a day editing videos. He built a TikTok automation agent using OpenClaw's **Browser Skill** and **Content Skill** in just one weekend.

## How It Works

The agent follows a simple 3-step pipeline:

- **Step 1: Trend Discovery** — The Browser Skill scans trending topics on TikTok, Twitter, and Reddit every 6 hours, extracting viral hooks and formats.
- **Step 2: Script Generation** — The Content Skill takes trending topics and generates 10-15 short video scripts daily, each optimized for TikTok's algorithm with hooks in the first 2 seconds.
- **Step 3: Batch Publishing** — Scripts are paired with stock footage and auto-published on a schedule, 4-5 videos per day across 3 accounts.

## Results

Within 5 days of launching:

- **500,000+ total views** across all accounts
- **12,000 new followers** gained organically
- **$1,000 MRR** from TikTok Creator Fund + affiliate links in bio
- Time spent dropped from **40 hours/week to 3 hours/week** (just reviewing scripts)

## Key Takeaways

> "The secret is not just automation — it's that OpenClaw's Content Skill actually understands what makes a TikTok hook work. I was skeptical, but the engagement rates are 3x what I got manually."

- Start with one niche before scaling to multiple accounts
- Always review scripts before publishing — the AI is good but not perfect
- Reinvest early revenue into better stock footage libraries
- Replicability: **4/5** — Requires basic understanding of TikTok trends but no coding skills
