## Background

GrowthMax is a small marketing consultancy that manages social media for local businesses. They were handling 5 clients manually — creating posts, scheduling, and engaging with comments across Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, and TikTok. With only 2 team members, scaling was impossible.

## The OpenClaw Setup

They built a multi-platform social media management agent:

- **Content Calendar**: The agent generates a 30-day content calendar based on each client's brand voice, industry trends, and top-performing past posts
- **Cross-Platform Adaptation**: One content idea is automatically adapted for each platform — short-form for Twitter, carousel for Instagram, professional tone for LinkedIn
- **Engagement Automation**: The agent monitors comments and DMs, replies to common questions, and flags important conversations for human follow-up
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Weekly performance reports are generated automatically with actionable insights

## Results (Over 3 Months)

- Average follower growth across all client accounts: **500%**
- Engagement rate increased by **320%**
- Content output went from **3 posts/week to 3 posts/day per platform**
- Team capacity expanded from **5 clients to 20 clients** without new hires
- Monthly revenue increased from **$3,000 to $12,000**

## What Worked Best

- The cross-platform adaptation is the killer feature — writing once and publishing everywhere with proper formatting saved enormous time
- Engagement automation handles 70% of comments, but the team reviews everything sensitive
- The content calendar feature reduced planning meetings from 2 hours/week per client to 15 minutes

> "We went from barely keeping up with 5 clients to confidently managing 20. OpenClaw didn't just save us time — it completely changed our business model."

## 背景

GrowthMax是一家小型营销咨询公司，为本地商户管理社交媒体。他们之前手动管理5个客户——在Twitter、Instagram、LinkedIn、Facebook和TikTok上创建帖子、排期和互动回复。团队只有2人，扩展是不可能的。

## OpenClaw搭建方案

他们搭建了一个多平台社交媒体管理Agent：

- **内容日历**：Agent根据每个客户的品牌调性、行业趋势和历史表现最佳帖子，生成30天内容日历
- **跨平台适配**：一个内容创意自动适配各平台——Twitter用短文案、Instagram用轮播图、LinkedIn用专业语气
- **互动自动化**：Agent监控评论和私信，回复常见问题，并将重要对话标记给人工跟进
- **数据分析仪表板**：每周自动生成带可执行洞察的效果报告

## 成果（3个月内）

- 所有客户账号的平均粉丝增长：**500%**
- 互动率提升**320%**
- 内容产出从**每平台每周3篇提升到每天3篇**
- 团队产能从**5个客户扩展到20个客户**，无需招人
- 月收入从**3000美元增长到12000美元**

## 什么最有效

- 跨平台适配是杀手级功能——写一次发布到所有平台并自动调整格式，节省了大量时间
- 互动自动化处理了70%的评论，但团队会审核所有敏感内容
- 内容日历功能将每个客户的规划会议从每周2小时减少到15分钟
