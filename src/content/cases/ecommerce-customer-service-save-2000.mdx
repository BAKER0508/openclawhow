## The Problem

Sarah runs a mid-sized Shopify store selling handmade candles. With 200+ customer messages daily across WhatsApp, Instagram DM, and email, she was spending $2,000/month on two part-time customer service reps.

## The OpenClaw Solution

She set up an OpenClaw agent with the **WhatsApp Skill** and **Knowledge Base Skill**:

- **Knowledge Base** — Uploaded her FAQ, return policy, shipping info, and product catalog. The agent can answer 90% of questions instantly.
- **WhatsApp Integration** — Connected to WhatsApp Business API. The agent replies within 30 seconds, 24/7.
- **Smart Escalation** — Complex issues (refunds, custom orders) are flagged and forwarded to Sarah with a summary.

## Results

- **99% response rate** within 1 minute (previously 4-6 hour average)
- **$2,000/month saved** in staffing costs
- Customer satisfaction score went from **3.8 to 4.7** out of 5
- **15% increase in repeat purchases** — fast replies build trust
- Only **8 messages/day** need human attention (down from 200+)

## Setup Details

Total setup time was about 4 hours:

- 1 hour: Writing and organizing the FAQ knowledge base
- 1 hour: Configuring the WhatsApp Business API connection
- 1 hour: Testing and tuning the agent's tone and escalation rules
- 1 hour: Setting up monitoring dashboard

> "I was terrified of letting a bot talk to my customers. But the replies are so natural that most people don't even realize it's automated. And my customers are actually happier now because they get instant answers."

## Replicability: 5/5

This is one of the easiest OpenClaw setups. If you have a product-based business with repetitive customer questions, you can replicate this in an afternoon.
