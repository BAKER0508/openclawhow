## Overview

This guide walks you through building an automated lead capture and follow-up system for real estate agents and agencies using OpenClaw. The system handles incoming inquiries from multiple channels, qualifies leads based on budget and timeline, schedules property viewings, and maintains personalized drip campaigns — so you never lose a deal to slow response times again.

## Top 5 Skills You Need

- **WhatsApp Skill** — Respond to property inquiries instantly on WhatsApp, the most common channel for real estate leads
- **CRM Skill** — Log and categorize every lead with budget, preferred area, timeline, and property type
- **Calendar Skill** — Book property viewings and open house slots directly from conversations
- **Email Skill** — Run automated drip campaigns with new listings that match each lead's criteria
- **Browser Skill** — Pull live listing data from MLS or your property management system

## Configuration Guide

### Step 1: Map Your Lead Sources

Identify and connect every channel where leads come in:

- Website contact forms and live chat widgets
- WhatsApp Business messages from listing ads
- Facebook and Instagram ad responses
- Zillow, Realtor.com, or local portal inquiries
- Referral and walk-in leads entered manually

### Step 2: Build Your Qualification Flow

Configure the AI to ask the right questions in the right order:

- Budget range and financing status (pre-approved, cash, exploring)
- Preferred neighborhoods and must-have features
- Timeline to purchase or lease (urgent, 1-3 months, exploring)
- Current living situation and motivation for moving
- Score each lead as hot, warm, or cold based on responses

### Step 3: Automate Follow-Up Sequences

Set up intelligent nurturing that feels personal:

- Hot leads get immediate viewing suggestions and agent callback within 1 hour
- Warm leads receive weekly curated listing emails matching their criteria
- Cold leads enter a monthly market update drip campaign
- Re-engagement triggers fire when a cold lead revisits your website or opens an email

### Step 4: Connect to Your Listing Database

Keep the AI informed about your inventory:

- Sync with MLS or your property management system daily
- Include pricing, photos, floor plans, and neighborhood data
- Set alerts when new listings match a lead's saved criteria
- Auto-remove sold or rented properties from recommendations

## Common Pitfalls

- **Don't let the AI negotiate pricing** — It should provide listing prices and comparables, but all negotiation must involve a licensed agent
- **Don't over-message leads** — More than 2 unreturned messages in a week feels pushy; back off and let the drip campaign work
- **Don't skip local compliance** — Real estate advertising rules vary by state and country; review all AI-generated messages for compliance
- **Don't ignore the handoff moment** — The transition from AI to human agent must be seamless; brief your agents on the full conversation history
- **Don't forget evenings and weekends** — Most property inquiries come outside business hours; this is where AI response speed matters most

## Expected Results

- 5x faster lead response time (under 2 minutes vs. industry average of 10+ hours)
- 40-60% increase in viewing bookings through instant scheduling
- 25% higher lead-to-client conversion rate with consistent follow-up
- 15+ hours saved per agent per week on repetitive inquiry handling

## 配置指南

### 第一步：梳理线索来源

识别并连接所有线索进入的渠道：

- 网站联系表单和在线聊天组件
- 来自房源广告的WhatsApp Business消息
- Facebook和Instagram广告回复
- Zillow、Realtor.com或本地平台询盘
- 推荐和上门客户手动录入

### 第二步：搭建资质确认流程

配置AI按正确的顺序提出正确的问题：

- 预算范围和融资状态（已预批、全款、考虑中）
- 首选社区和必须功能
- 购房或租房时间线（紧急、1-3个月、考虑中）
- 当前居住状况和搬家动机
- 根据回答将每条线索评分为热线索、温线索或冷线索

### 第三步：自动化跟进序列

搭建感觉个性化的智能培育流程：

- 热线索立即获得看房建议，经纪人1小时内回电
- 温线索每周收到匹配其条件的精选房源邮件
- 冷线索进入月度市场动态滴灌营销
- 冷线索重访网站或打开邮件时触发再激活流程

### 第四步：连接房源数据库

让AI实时掌握你的房源：

- 每日同步MLS或你的房产管理系统
- 包含价格、照片、户型图和社区数据
- 新房源匹配线索已保存条件时设置提醒
- 已售或已租房产自动从推荐中移除

## 常见陷阱

- **不要让AI议价**——它应提供挂牌价和比较数据，但所有谈判必须有持证经纪人参与
- **不要过度发消息**——一周内超过2条未回复消息会让人觉得烦人；退后一步让滴灌营销发挥作用
- **不要忽视当地合规**——房产广告规则因州和国家而异；审查所有AI生成的消息是否合规
- **不要忽视交接时刻**——从AI到人工经纪人的过渡必须无缝；向经纪人简报完整对话历史
- **不要忘记晚间和周末**——大多数房产询盘在工作时间之外；这正是AI响应速度最重要的时候
