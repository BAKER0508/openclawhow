## Overview

This guide shows you how to build an AI-powered tutoring and course creation system using OpenClaw. Whether you run an online learning platform, offer private tutoring, or create educational content, this solution automates personalized learning paths, quiz generation, student progress tracking, and course material production — freeing you to focus on high-value teaching moments.

## Top 5 Skills You Need

- **Knowledge Base Skill** — Upload textbooks, curricula, and learning materials so the AI delivers accurate, curriculum-aligned answers
- **Chat Skill** — Provide real-time conversational tutoring with adaptive difficulty based on student responses
- **Google Sheets Skill** — Track student progress, scores, and learning milestones in structured spreadsheets
- **PDF Generator Skill** — Auto-generate worksheets, study guides, and course handouts from your content library
- **Scheduler Skill** — Manage tutoring session bookings and send automated reminders to students

## Configuration Guide

### Step 1: Structure Your Learning Content

Organize your teaching materials into clear categories:

- Core subject matter broken into modules and lessons
- Difficulty levels (beginner, intermediate, advanced) for each topic
- Common student misconceptions and how to address them
- Practice questions with detailed answer explanations
- Supplementary reading and video resources

### Step 2: Build the Tutoring Agent

Configure the AI tutor with the right personality and guardrails:

- Set a patient, encouraging teaching tone that adapts to student level
- Enable Socratic questioning mode — guide students to answers rather than giving them directly
- Connect the Knowledge Base Skill with your structured curriculum
- Add fallback responses for topics outside your scope

### Step 3: Set Up Progress Tracking

Automate how you monitor student performance:

- Use Google Sheets Skill to log every quiz score and session summary
- Create automated weekly progress reports sent to students or parents
- Set triggers for intervention when a student scores below 60% on three consecutive quizzes
- Track time-on-task metrics to identify engagement drops

### Step 4: Automate Course Material Production

Let the AI help you scale content creation:

- Generate practice quizzes from your lesson content automatically
- Create personalized study plans based on individual student weaknesses
- Produce formatted PDF worksheets and flashcard sets on demand
- A/B test different explanation styles and track which ones improve comprehension

## Common Pitfalls

- **Don't replace human judgment on struggling students** — AI can flag at-risk learners, but a real teacher should handle sensitive conversations about performance
- **Don't trust AI-generated content without review** — Always verify factual accuracy of generated quizzes and study materials before distributing
- **Don't make the AI too helpful** — Students learn best when they struggle productively; configure the tutor to give hints before full answers
- **Don't ignore accessibility** — Ensure generated materials work for students with different learning needs and reading levels
- **Don't skip the onboarding flow** — New students need a brief assessment to calibrate the AI tutor's starting difficulty

## Expected Results

- 70% reduction in time spent creating quizzes, worksheets, and study guides
- 3x more students served per tutor with AI handling routine Q&A
- 20-35% improvement in student test scores through personalized practice
- 90%+ student satisfaction rate when AI tutoring is combined with periodic human check-ins

## 配置指南

### 第一步：结构化你的学习内容

将教学材料整理成清晰的分类：

- 按模块和课时拆分的核心科目内容
- 每个主题的难度级别（初级、中级、高级）
- 常见学生误区及如何纠正
- 带详细答案解析的练习题
- 补充阅读和视频资源

### 第二步：搭建辅导Agent

配置AI家教的性格和边界：

- 设置耐心、鼓励性的教学语气，根据学生水平自适应
- 启用苏格拉底式提问模式——引导学生找到答案而非直接给出
- 将知识库技能与结构化课程连接
- 为超出范围的话题添加兜底回复

### 第三步：搭建进度追踪

自动化监控学生表现：

- 使用Google Sheets技能记录每次测验成绩和课程总结
- 创建自动每周进度报告发送给学生或家长
- 设置触发器：当学生连续三次测验低于60分时发出干预提醒
- 追踪学习时长指标以识别参与度下降

### 第四步：自动化课程材料制作

让AI帮你扩大内容生产规模：

- 从课程内容自动生成练习试题
- 基于每个学生的薄弱环节创建个性化学习计划
- 按需生成格式化的PDF练习册和闪卡集
- A/B测试不同的讲解风格，追踪哪种能提升理解力

## 常见陷阱

- **不要在学困生问题上替代人工判断**——AI可以标记高风险学生，但关于学习表现的敏感对话应由真人老师处理
- **不要不审核就信任AI生成的内容**——分发前始终验证试题和学习材料的事实准确性
- **不要让AI太"有帮助"**——学生在适度挣扎中学得最好；配置AI先给提示再给完整答案
- **不要忽视可及性**——确保生成的材料适用于不同学习需求和阅读水平的学生
- **不要跳过入门评估**——新学生需要简短的测评来校准AI家教的起始难度
