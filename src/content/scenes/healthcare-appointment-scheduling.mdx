## Overview

This guide shows you how to build an automated appointment scheduling and patient FAQ system for clinics, dental offices, and small hospitals using OpenClaw. The system handles appointment booking, rescheduling, cancellations, insurance questions, and pre-visit instructions — reducing front desk workload while improving patient access to care.

## Top 5 Skills You Need

- **Calendar Skill** — Manage doctor availability, book appointments, and handle rescheduling across multiple providers
- **Knowledge Base Skill** — Store clinic policies, insurance accepted, preparation instructions, and common medical FAQs
- **WhatsApp Skill** — Communicate with patients on their preferred messaging channel for bookings and reminders
- **Email Skill** — Send appointment confirmations, pre-visit instructions, and follow-up care summaries
- **Notification Skill** — Alert staff when urgent cases need triage or when patients require special accommodations

## Configuration Guide

### Step 1: Map Your Provider Availability

Set up accurate scheduling data:

- Each doctor's working hours, lunch breaks, and days off
- Appointment types and their durations (15-min follow-up, 30-min new patient, 60-min procedure)
- Buffer time between appointments for cleanup or documentation
- Holiday schedules and planned vacation blocks
- Emergency slots reserved for same-day urgent cases

### Step 2: Build the Patient-Facing FAQ

Cover the questions that flood your front desk daily:

- Insurance plans accepted and billing procedures
- What to bring to a first visit (ID, insurance card, medication list)
- Preparation instructions for specific procedures (fasting, stopping medications)
- Parking directions, public transit options, and accessibility info
- Telehealth availability and how to join virtual appointments

### Step 3: Configure the Booking Flow

Design a smooth scheduling experience:

- Ask for patient type (new or returning) and reason for visit
- Show available slots filtered by doctor, date preference, and appointment type
- Collect necessary pre-visit information (symptoms, medication changes)
- Send instant confirmation with date, time, location, and preparation checklist
- Schedule automated reminders at 48 hours and 2 hours before the appointment

### Step 4: Set Up Safety Guardrails

Healthcare requires extra caution:

- Never provide medical diagnoses or treatment recommendations
- Flag emergency symptoms (chest pain, difficulty breathing) with immediate escalation to staff
- Route complex insurance disputes to billing department
- Log all patient interactions for compliance and audit purposes
- Ensure HIPAA-compatible data handling in all integrations

## Common Pitfalls

- **Never let the AI give medical advice** — It books appointments and answers logistics questions only; clinical judgment stays with providers
- **Don't skip no-show management** — Automate waitlist backfill when cancellations happen; empty slots cost real revenue
- **Don't ignore time zone issues** — If you serve patients across regions, always confirm time zone during booking
- **Don't forget language accessibility** — Configure multilingual support if your patient population needs it
- **Don't underestimate rescheduling volume** — 30-40% of patients reschedule; make this flow as smooth as initial booking

## Expected Results

- 60% reduction in front desk phone call volume
- 35% decrease in no-show rates through automated reminders
- Under 30-second average booking time vs. 4-5 minutes by phone
- 90%+ patient satisfaction with the self-service scheduling experience

## 配置指南

### 第一步：配置医生排班

搭建准确的排班数据：

- 每位医生的工作时间、午休和休息日
- 预约类型及其时长（15分钟复诊、30分钟初诊、60分钟手术）
- 预约间的缓冲时间用于清洁或记录
- 节假日安排和计划休假
- 为当日紧急情况预留的急诊时段

### 第二步：搭建患者端FAQ

覆盖每天涌入前台的常见问题：

- 接受的医保计划和账单流程
- 初次就诊需要携带的材料（身份证、医保卡、用药清单）
- 特定检查的准备说明（禁食、停药）
- 停车指引、公共交通选项和无障碍信息
- 远程医疗的可用性和加入虚拟问诊的方式

### 第三步：配置预约流程

设计流畅的挂号体验：

- 询问患者类型（新患还是复诊）和就诊原因
- 按医生、日期偏好和预约类型筛选可用时段
- 收集必要的就诊前信息（症状、用药变化）
- 即时发送确认，包含日期、时间、地点和准备清单
- 在预约前48小时和2小时自动发送提醒

### 第四步：搭建安全防线

医疗领域需要格外谨慎：

- 绝不提供医学诊断或治疗建议
- 标记紧急症状（胸痛、呼吸困难）并立即转接工作人员
- 将复杂的医保纠纷路由到账单部门
- 记录所有患者交互用于合规和审计
- 确保所有集成中的数据处理符合HIPAA规范

## 常见陷阱

- **绝不让AI给出医疗建议**——它只负责预约挂号和回答后勤问题；临床判断由医生负责
- **不要忽视爽约管理**——取消时自动回填候补名单；空余时段意味着实际收入损失
- **不要忽视时区问题**——如果你服务跨地区的患者，预约时始终确认时区
- **不要忘记语言无障碍**——如果你的患者群体需要，配置多语言支持
- **不要低估改约量**——30-40%的患者会改约；让这个流程和首次预约一样顺畅

- 前台电话量减少60%
- 通过自动提醒，爽约率降低35%
- 平均预约时间不到30秒，对比电话预约的4-5分钟
