## Overview

This guide walks you through setting up a complete customer service automation system for your e-commerce store using OpenClaw. Whether you sell on Shopify, WooCommerce, or your own platform, this solution handles 90%+ of customer inquiries automatically.

## Top 5 Skills You Need

- **WhatsApp Skill** — Connect to WhatsApp Business API for instant customer messaging
- **Knowledge Base Skill** — Upload your FAQ, policies, and product info for accurate answers
- **Browser Skill** — Check order status, tracking numbers, and inventory in real-time
- **Email Skill** — Handle email support tickets with the same AI agent
- **Notification Skill** — Alert your team when human intervention is needed

## Configuration Guide

### Step 1: Build Your Knowledge Base

Start by documenting everything your customers ask about:

- Shipping times and costs for each region
- Return and refund policies (be specific about timelines)
- Product specifications and sizing guides
- Payment methods and security information
- Common troubleshooting steps

### Step 2: Set Up Communication Channels

Connect the agent to your customer touchpoints:

- WhatsApp Business (most common for D2C brands)
- Email inbox (for formal inquiries)
- Website live chat (optional but recommended)

### Step 3: Configure Escalation Rules

Not everything should be automated. Set clear rules for when to involve a human:

- Refund requests over $100
- Complaints mentioning legal action
- Repeated contacts from the same customer (3+ times)
- Custom product requests
- Any message the AI confidence is below 70%

### Step 4: Test Extensively

Before going live, test with at least 50 sample conversations covering edge cases.

## Common Pitfalls

- **Don't skip the knowledge base** — The agent is only as good as the information you give it
- **Don't automate refunds** — Always require human approval for money-back transactions
- **Monitor the first week closely** — Check every conversation and tune the agent's responses
- **Set customer expectations** — Let customers know they can request a human agent at any time
- **Update your knowledge base monthly** — Products change, policies evolve

## Expected Results

- 90%+ automated response rate
- Under 1 minute average response time (vs. industry average of 4+ hours)
- 30-50% reduction in customer service costs
- Higher customer satisfaction due to instant, consistent answers
