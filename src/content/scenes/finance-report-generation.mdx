## Overview

This guide shows you how to build an automated financial reporting and data analysis system using OpenClaw. Whether you manage reporting for a startup, a finance consultancy, or an internal FP&A team, this solution pulls data from multiple sources, generates formatted reports, flags anomalies, and delivers insights on schedule — eliminating the spreadsheet grind that consumes most analysts' weeks.

## Top 5 Skills You Need

- **Google Sheets Skill** — Connect to financial spreadsheets, pull transaction data, and write computed summaries back automatically
- **Browser Skill** — Scrape market data, exchange rates, and public financial filings for up-to-date analysis
- **PDF Generator Skill** — Produce polished financial reports, dashboards, and executive summaries in client-ready format
- **Email Skill** — Distribute reports to stakeholders on a scheduled basis with personalized cover notes
- **Notification Skill** — Send real-time alerts when key metrics cross predefined thresholds (cash burn, revenue dip, expense spike)

## Configuration Guide

### Step 1: Connect Your Data Sources

Identify and integrate every data input your reports need:

- Accounting software exports (QuickBooks, Xero, or bank CSVs)
- Revenue data from payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
- Expense data from corporate card platforms and reimbursement tools
- Market benchmarks and competitor data from public sources
- Budget vs. actual figures from your planning spreadsheets

### Step 2: Define Your Report Templates

Standardize what gets produced and when:

- Monthly P&L summary with variance analysis against budget
- Weekly cash flow snapshot with 4-week rolling forecast
- Quarterly board deck with KPI trends, burn rate, and runway
- Ad-hoc deep dives triggered by anomaly alerts
- Year-over-year comparison reports for annual planning

### Step 3: Configure Anomaly Detection

Set up intelligent alerting so you catch problems early:

- Revenue drops exceeding 15% week-over-week
- Expense categories exceeding monthly budget by more than 20%
- Cash runway falling below 6 months based on current burn rate
- Unusual transaction patterns (duplicate charges, vendor spikes)
- Missing data or delayed imports that could compromise report accuracy

### Step 4: Automate Distribution and Archiving

Get reports to the right people without manual effort:

- Schedule email delivery: weekly reports every Monday at 8 AM, monthly on the 2nd
- Customize report depth by audience (executive summary vs. detailed analyst view)
- Archive all generated reports in a shared drive with consistent naming conventions
- Track who opens reports and follow up if key stakeholders don't review within 48 hours

## Common Pitfalls

- **Don't skip data validation** — Garbage in, garbage out; always verify source data accuracy before generating reports
- **Don't automate without an audit trail** — Log every data pull, transformation, and calculation for compliance and debugging
- **Don't over-rely on AI narrative generation** — AI can describe trends, but a human analyst should interpret causes and recommend actions
- **Don't ignore currency and time zone issues** — Multi-entity or international businesses need explicit handling of FX conversions
- **Don't set and forget** — Review report templates quarterly; business metrics evolve and reports should evolve with them

## Expected Results

- 80% reduction in time spent compiling monthly financial reports
- Reports delivered 2-3 days earlier each month, improving decision speed
- 95%+ accuracy in automated calculations vs. error-prone manual spreadsheets
- 50% fewer ad-hoc data requests from stakeholders thanks to proactive alerting

## 配置指南

### 第一步：连接数据源

识别并整合报告所需的每个数据输入：

- 会计软件导出（QuickBooks、Xero或银行CSV）
- 支付处理器的收入数据（Stripe、PayPal）
- 企业信用卡平台和报销工具的费用数据
- 公开来源的市场基准和竞品数据
- 规划电子表格中的预算与实际对比数据

### 第二步：定义报告模板

标准化生成什么报告以及何时生成：

- 月度损益表摘要，含与预算的差异分析
- 每周现金流快照，含4周滚动预测
- 季度董事会报告，含KPI趋势、消耗率和跑道
- 由异常告警触发的临时深度分析
- 用于年度规划的同比对比报告

### 第三步：配置异常检测

搭建智能告警以尽早发现问题：

- 收入周环比下降超过15%
- 费用类别超过月度预算20%以上
- 按当前消耗率计算现金跑道低于6个月
- 异常交易模式（重复扣款、供应商费用飙升）
- 数据缺失或导入延迟可能影响报告准确性

### 第四步：自动化分发和归档

无需手动操作即可将报告送达对的人：

- 排定邮件发送：周报每周一早8点，月报每月2日
- 按受众定制报告深度（高管摘要vs详细分析版）
- 所有生成的报告按统一命名规则归档到共享盘
- 追踪谁打开了报告，关键利益相关者48小时内未查看则跟进

## 常见陷阱

- **不要跳过数据验证**——垃圾进垃圾出；生成报告前始终验证源数据准确性
- **不要自动化而不留审计轨迹**——记录每次数据拉取、转换和计算，用于合规和调试
- **不要过度依赖AI叙事生成**——AI能描述趋势，但人工分析师应负责解读原因和建议行动
- **不要忽视货币和时区问题**——多实体或跨国企业需要明确处理汇率转换
- **不要设置后就不管了**——每季度审查报告模板；业务指标在变，报告也应随之调整
