## Overview

This scene solution shows you how to build an AI-powered content creation workflow using OpenClaw. It covers everything from topic research to final publishing, designed for solo creators and small content teams.

## The Workflow Pipeline

### Stage 1: Topic Research

Use the **Browser Skill** to automatically scan:

- Trending topics on Twitter, Reddit, and Hacker News
- Top-performing competitor content
- Google Trends data for your niche
- Questions people ask on Quora and StackOverflow

The agent compiles a weekly list of 20-30 topic ideas ranked by potential traffic and relevance.

### Stage 2: Outline Generation

For each approved topic, the **Content Skill** generates:

- A detailed article outline with H2 and H3 headings
- Key points to cover under each section
- Suggested word count (based on top-ranking competitors)
- Internal linking opportunities to your existing content

### Stage 3: Draft Writing

The agent writes first drafts following your brand voice guidelines:

- Maintains consistent tone across all content
- Includes relevant statistics and examples
- Adds natural keyword placement for SEO
- Generates meta titles and descriptions

### Stage 4: Review and Polish

Human review is essential at this stage:

- Check facts and statistics
- Add personal anecdotes or unique insights
- Ensure the content matches your brand voice
- Verify all links work

### Stage 5: Multi-Platform Publishing

The agent adapts and publishes your content across platforms:

- Full article to your blog (WordPress, Ghost, etc.)
- Thread version for Twitter
- Summary post for LinkedIn
- Short-form adaptation for Instagram or TikTok

## Skills Used

- **Browser Skill** — Research and trend discovery
- **Content Skill** — Writing and adaptation
- **SEO Skill** — Keyword analysis and optimization
- **Publishing Skill** — Cross-platform distribution

## Best Practices

- Always review AI-generated drafts before publishing
- Feed the agent examples of your best content to train the tone
- Start with 2-3 articles per week, scale up gradually
- Track performance metrics to see which AI-assisted topics perform best
- Keep a human touch in at least 20% of the content for authenticity

## Expected Output

- 5-10x more content output with the same team size
- 50% reduction in time from idea to published article
- Consistent quality across all pieces
- Better SEO coverage due to systematic keyword targeting
